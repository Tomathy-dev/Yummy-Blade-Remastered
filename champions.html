Champion Roles

Although every champion is unique, we can categorize champion into a spectrum of 6 roles. Some champions are not bound by only one role but can share the characteristics of multile roles.

Now let's explore the 6 current champion roles:
Mages- Mages are powerfull spell caster who us their abilities to inflict magic damage and disrupt enemies. They usually have low defense stats to balance out their huge offensive capability.
Marksman- Marksmen or Attack Damage Carries (ADC's) primarly use their ranged auto-attacks to show their damage output. They focus on inflicting the most damage per second (DPS) on a single target. The downside is that by betting on a full damage/DPS build, ADC's become easy targets to kill (due to their "squishiness"), so these types of champions need to be constantly repositioning in a fight so enemies don't get the chance to bully them.
Tanks- Tanks are usually melee champions who, as the name might suggest, have very high defensive stats. But, contradictory to the name, have a low damage output. Their job is to disrupt and disable enemy champions while soaking up damage from the other team. They are normally the front-liners of a battle, with the purpose of protecting their own team.
Supports- Supports help allies by shielding, healing or empowering them. They excell at utility, providing assistance to teammates, at the exchange of low damage dealing.
Fighters- Fighters are typically melee champions with the ability to not only inflict a good portion of attack damage but also be able to tank some damage from enemies. These kinds of champions are usually perfect for dueling, since they have the perfect balance between damage output and survivability.
Assassins- Assassins are champions with a high mobility kit and capable of inflicting tons of damage in the lowest amount of time possible (burst damage). The downside is the high risk these characters take. If your burst damage is wasted, it's extremely hard for an assassin to then duel or escape fights with enemies.
