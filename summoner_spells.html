Summoner Spells

Summoner Spells are spells that are chosen during Champion Select(or in-game, with the Spellbook Rune), and do not depend on the Champion that is picked.

Although Summoner Spells are not restricted by the Champion you select, the value you get out of them varies greatly. You need to keep in mind your Champion, the role and position you are occupying, and lastly, your team and your enemy's Champions.

Using Summoner Spells Effectively

As we already said above, the value of Summoner Spells varies greatly due to team compositions, but there are ways to improve their value regardless of the situation.

Flash is the most used Summoner Spell due to its high versatility. It is almost regarded as mandatory, with a recent official survey showing that it is used 96% of the time. It can be used to dodge skillshots, cancel some abilities' animations, re-position, land abilities, escape, chase and much more. The best way to use it is usually to cross terrain with it, effectively travelling more units than its pre-determined range. To do so, you need to flash more than half of the wall. Technically you can flash up to 898 Units with this trick.

Ignite is mostly used to finish off enemies (especially in the early game) but sometimes that is not the most effective way. The Grievous Wounds effect should be taken advantage off against targets with either a lot of lifesteal or HP regeneration. Lastly, you can ignite earlier than usual to get the enemy below 40% HP so that, combined with the Coup De Grace Rune, you can deal more damage.

Heal should not simply be used when you or an ally are low HP. When using it defensively, you should use it like Barrier, that is, to mitigate incoming damage. This is to bait out the enemies into committing, and potentially turn out victorious.

Teleport should not just be used to get to lane faster, but to have more map presence, and to be able to Split Push (pushing a lane alone, while your team has other objectives on sight).
